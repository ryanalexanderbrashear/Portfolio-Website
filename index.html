My Journey

  •

    August 2009-May 2013

    University of Pikeville

    For the undergraduate portion of my college education, I attended the University of Pikeville in Pikeville, Kentucky. I graduated with a Bachelor of Science degree, majoring in Computer Science and minoring in Mathematics.

  • August 2013-December 2014

    Eastern Kentucky University

    Continuing my education post-graduation, I attended Eastern Kentucky University, where I graduated with a Master of Science degree in Applied Computing with a concentration in Software Engineering and Computer Security.

  • September 2016-January 2017

    TEKY Program

    Searching for employment in eastern Kentucky, I heard about the pilot TechHire Eastern Kentucky program with Interapt, in Paintsville, Kentucky, and decided to attend.

  • January 2017-January 2018

    iOS Developer & Curriculum Creator

    Successfully graduating from the TEKY program, I was hired on by Interapt as an iOS development intern, and later moved into the position of junior iOS developer. Due to the combination of my experience as a developer and having gone through the TEKY program, I was then brought on board to help write the new Interapt Skills iOS curriculum.

  • January 2018-August 2018

    First Steps into Teaching

    Starting in January 2018, I worked as an iOS teaching assistant for the Interapt Skills program with JCPS. Following the end of that program, I taught iOS to a small group of students as the instructor for the Interapt Skills iOS summer cohort.

  • August 2018-Present

    Barren County Interapt Skills

    In August 2018, the Barren County Interapt Skills program began in Glasgow, Kentucky, with me serving as the lead iOS instructor.
